Services

We offer a full suite of strategic finance services designed for small to mid-sized businesses. Engagement scope can be hourly, monthly retainer, or project-based.

Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A)

Turn your numbers into a strategic roadmap.

  • Monthly and quarterly financial models tailored to your business drivers.
  • Scenario planning and sensitivity analysis to prepare for multiple outcomes.
  • Management reporting packages (revenue, margin, ops KPIs) for leadership and board meetings.
  • Actionable insights to improve margins and operational efficiency.

Forecasting & Budgeting

Reliable forecasts that align operations with strategy.

  • Rolling 12- to 24-month forecasts with clear assumptions and triggers.
  • Annual budgeting support including departmental collaboration and variance analysis.
  • Forecast governance so your team maintains disciplined updates and accountability.

Cash Flow Management

Protect runway and improve working capital.

  • Cash flow forecasting (daily/weekly/monthly) and runway models.
  • Working capital improvement plans: AR, AP, inventory, and collections strategies.
  • Short-term liquidity plans and recommendations for bridge financing or cost restructuring.

Budgeting & Cost Control

Establish financial discipline without stifling growth.

  • Zero-based and driver-based budgeting approaches depending on business needs.
  • Expense categorization and cost optimization initiatives.
  • KPIs and dashboards to measure budget adherence and ROI of expenditures.

Strategic Financial Advisory

High-level financial strategy and execution support.

  • Capital raise preparation (investor decks, financial due diligence readiness).
  • M&A support: target evaluation, valuation modeling, and integration planning.
  • Pricing strategy, margin optimization, and go-to-market financial modeling.
  • Interim financial leadership — stand in as your CFO when needed.

Engagement Deliverables — What to Expect

  • Clear engagement plan and milestones within the first 30 days.
  • Actionable monthly reports and a prioritized improvement roadmap.
  • Hand-over documentation and training to internal teams for sustainable progress.
